Manic Mansion

Your car broke down and your only hope is a ghastly mansion in the middle of the woods. Upon entering the house and touching the crystal inside something happens... The player will find themselves diving into 3 very distinct levels, themed after the room that they find each portal in. Can you get to the end of each level, collecting crystal pieces along the way to rebuild the crystal and leave this accursed mansion?

Engine: Unity 3D

Finding Rachel

You play as a park ranger acting on a missing persons report to find a young girl named Rachel who got lost in the forest. On your journey to find her you see that there are portals scattered around the environment. Each portal descends you further into madness. Each level brings new challenges that you must complete to find Rachel. Can you find Rachel as you descend into madness?
