Waterford City Route Finder

This app allows the user to pick two or more points and it will calculate the shortest route using Breath-First Search algorithm. The user can also define a point that they want the route to include or have the app calculate up to 3 shortest routes from point A to point B.
The app also contains a collection of landmarks present around Waterford City. The user can choose to pick the cheapest (shortest in kms) path between chosen landmarks. They can also choose the most historic path and a path that specifically avoids a certain landmark. The landmarks are all displayed in a TreeView object in the GUI.

Purpose: College Project
Languages and Tools Involved: Java, Javafx, Intellij IDEA
Date Published: 13th May 2021
Authors: Jakub Poczatek & Caolan Maher

Fruit Analyzer

The fruit analyser will take in an imagine and detect fruit present in the image. It will first convert the image into a black and white image, colouring in the fruit as white. It will then create sets and use these sets to draw boxes around the fruits on the image. By clicking on these fruits you can also view some basic stats about it. The app also has a second window with a black & white image, the fruits on this image can be coloured in by random colours with the press of a button. Currently the app supports Strawberries, Oranges and Cherries.

Purpose: College Project
Languages & Tools involved: Java, Javafx, Intellij IDEA
Date Published: 24th March 2021
Authors: Jakub Poczatek

Pokedex App

Pokedex is a web app that lets you maintain your own pokemon collection. You can add different generations and inside the generations you can add specific pokemon to keep your collection nice and organised. It allows you to specify the name, pokedex number, type, rarity and image of the pokemon and lets you edit these parameters at will.
The Home Page will show you your personal stats as well as some global stats that are based on the total number of generations and pokemon added by all users on the system.
The "Our Staff" page will display some information on a made up team behind the web page, an embedded google map pointing to the nintendo offices in Japan and gives you the ability to leave comments that will be visible by all users.

Purpose: College Project
Languages & Tools Involved: HTML, CSS, JavaScript,
Node.js, ExpressJS, Handlebars & Glitch
